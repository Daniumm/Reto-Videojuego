Our project

This is an eduacational videogame that teaches the sound of different percussive instruments from cultures around the world.

Motivation

We love music and also technology, what if we created something with our knowledge? This is how the project started. We would like to share something and at the same time have fun!

Objectives

One of our main goals is to provide a great user experience and leave something valuable in our players. And not only that, but share instruments from around the world and other cultures is an important objective as well.

Transparency

Because we know that not everyone has access to game and web development education, we share our process and source files. Also, this means that is an open source project and everyone can contribute.
