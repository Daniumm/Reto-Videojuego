About our game

Each level represents a region of the world, in which different percussive instruments, that I bet you don't know, are presented along with the character of the region. Before you start playing you can spit out all the instruments of the level to familiarize yourself with them. Once the game is started, you have 3 opportunities to hear the sound and, depending on the level, you have limited attempts to guess which instrument that sound belongs to. If you answer correctly, you will gain a point, on the other hand, if your answer is incorrect, you will lose a life. In the first level you have 5 lives, in the second 4, in the third 3 and so on. If the player gets a certain amount of points, the user will be able to move on to the next level.

Our project

This is an eduacational videogame that teaches the sound of different percussive instruments from cultures around the world.

Motivation

We love music and also technology, what if we created something with our knowledge? This is how the project started. We would like to share something and at the same time have fun!

Objectives

One of our main goals is to provide a great user experience and leave something valuable in our players. And not only that, but share instruments from around the world and other cultures is an important objective as well.

Transparency

Because we know that not everyone has access to game and web development education, we share our process and source files. Also, this means that is an open source project and everyone can contribute.
